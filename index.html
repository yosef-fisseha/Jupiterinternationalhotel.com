Welcome to Jupiter International Hotel

A close-knit group of two hotels, the Jupiter Group thrives to give its guests a contemporary take on luxury living. With two hotels in the capital of Ethiopia; Jupiter International Hotel Cazanchise and Jupiter International Hotel Bole, our hotels are positioned at proximity to shopping destinations, sightseeing spots and travel hubs in the city of Addis Ababa.

Our establishments provide the best of top-line services and facilities that suit the needs of both business and leisure travellers. They abide by international standards when it comes to safety, hospitality and accommodation, to make sure that all our guests enjoy a stay of utmost comfort.
